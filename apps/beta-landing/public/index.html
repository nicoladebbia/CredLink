Frequently Asked Questions

What is C2PA?

C2PA (Coalition for Content Provenance and Authenticity) is an open standard for cryptographically signing digital content. It's backed by Adobe, Microsoft, BBC, and other major organizations.

How long does beta last?

Beta program runs for 3 months. You'll get 30 days notice before any pricing changes.

What happens to my data?

We store cryptographic proofs, not your images. Images are processed in-memory and immediately discarded. Proofs are stored for verification purposes.

Can I verify images signed by others?

Yes! Any C2PA-signed image can be verified, regardless of who signed it.

What's the API rate limit?

No limits during beta. Production will have generous limits based on your plan.

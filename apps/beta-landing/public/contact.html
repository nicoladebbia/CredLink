Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I receive a response?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. Technical support issues are prioritized and usually receive responses within 4-6 hours. Enterprise customers receive dedicated 24/7 support.

What information should I include in my support request?

For technical support, please include: your API key (or account email), detailed description of the issue, error messages, steps to reproduce, and expected vs actual behavior. For general inquiries, include your company name and specific questions.

Do you offer phone support?

Yes! We offer phone support for all customers. Enterprise customers receive 24/7 dedicated phone support. Standard customers can reach us during business hours (9 AM - 6 PM PST). Emergency technical support is available 24/7 for critical issues.

How do I report a security vulnerability?

For security vulnerabilities, please email us at security@credlink.com or call our emergency security line. We take all security reports seriously and will respond within 2 hours. Please do not publicly disclose vulnerabilities until we've had a chance to address them.

Can I schedule a demo or consultation?

Absolutely! Contact our sales team to schedule a personalized demo. We offer both online and in-person consultations depending on your location and needs. Enterprise customers can request on-site visits and technical workshops.

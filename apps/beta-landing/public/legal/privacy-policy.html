1. Introduction

CredLink is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use our content authenticity platform and services.

Our mission is to provide trustworthy content provenance while maintaining the highest standards of privacy and data protection. This policy reflects our commitment to transparency and user control over personal data.

We adhere to GDPR, CCPA, and other major privacy regulations worldwide. Our platform is designed with privacy-by-design principles, ensuring that personal data protection is built into every aspect of our service.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, company, and role when you register for an account
  • API Keys: Authentication credentials used to access our services
  • Image Metadata: Creator information, titles, descriptions, and other metadata you choose to include with signed content
  • Communications: Messages you send to our support team or participate in our community forums

Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: API requests, signing/verification operations, and service usage patterns
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and access timestamps
  • Performance Data: Response times, error rates, and system performance metrics
  • Security Data: Authentication attempts, access logs, and security-related events

Content-Related Data

  • Image Data: The actual image files you submit for signing (processed and stored securely)
  • C2PA Manifests: Content authenticity manifests and provenance data
  • Verification Results: Outcomes of content verification processes
  • Audit Trails: Records of all signing and verification operations

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Access Controls: Role-based access control with principle of least privilege
  • Secure Infrastructure: Cloud-based infrastructure with regular security audits
  • Monitoring: 24/7 security monitoring and threat detection

Organizational Security Measures

  • Employee Training: Regular privacy and security training for all employees
  • Background Checks: Comprehensive background checks for personnel with data access
  • Privacy Policies: Internal privacy policies and procedures
  • Incident Response: Established procedures for security incident response

5. Data Retention

Retention Periods

  • Account Information: Retained while your account is active
  • Image Data: Retained for 90 days after signing, unless you choose longer retention
  • C2PA Manifests: Retained indefinitely to ensure ongoing verification capability
  • Audit Logs: Retained for 7 years for compliance and security purposes
  • Usage Analytics: Retained for 2 years in anonymized form

Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your personal data at any time through your account settings or by contacting our privacy team. We will delete your data within 30 days of your request, subject to legal and operational requirements.

‚ö†Ô∏è Important Retention Note

C2PA manifests and verification records cannot be completely deleted as they are essential for maintaining the integrity of the content authenticity ecosystem. However, personal information associated with these records can be anonymized upon request.

6. Your Privacy Rights

GDPR Rights (EU Residents)

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to Restriction: Limit how we process your data
  • Right to Object: Object to certain types of processing

CCPA Rights (California Residents)

  • Right to Know: Understand what personal information is collected
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt-out of sale of personal information
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: Not receive discriminatory treatment for exercising privacy rights

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact our Privacy Officer using the information provided in the "Contact Information" section below. We will respond to your request within 30 days (or as required by applicable law).

8. International Data Transfers

CredLink operates globally and may transfer your personal data across international borders. We ensure appropriate protection for your data through:

  • Adequacy Decisions: Transfers to countries with EU-recognized adequacy decisions
  • Standard Contractual Clauses: EU-approved standard contractual clauses for other transfers
  • Binding Corporate Rules: Internal rules for intra-organizational transfers

Our primary data centers are located in the United States and European Union, ensuring compliance with regional data protection requirements.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under the age of 13 (or applicable age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will take steps to delete that information immediately.

Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided us with personal information should contact our Privacy Officer immediately.

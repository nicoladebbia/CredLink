## 1. Definitions

**"C2PA"** means Coalition for Content Provenance and Authenticity, including its technical specifications for content provenance and authenticity.

**"C2PA-as-a-Service"** means the Provider's cloud-based service that enables Customers to generate, sign, verify, and manage C2PA manifests for digital content, including remote manifest survival, evidence custody, and compliance reporting.

**"Content"** means any digital asset, file, or media that Customer processes through the Service, including images, videos, audio, documents, and other digital content.

**"Manifest"** means a C2PA-compliant assertion bundle that contains cryptographic proof of content provenance, authenticity, and metadata.

**"Remote Manifest Survival"** means the Provider's guarantee that C2PA manifests remain accessible, verifiable, and cryptographically valid for the specified retention period.

**"Evidence"** means compliance-related data, logs, timestamps, and audit trails generated by the Service, including TSA receipts, badge logs, and verification records.

**"WORM"** means Write-Once-Read-Many storage that prevents modification or deletion of evidence before the specified retention period expires.

**"Service Credits"** means credits applied to Customer's future invoice amounts as specified in the SLA.

## 2. Services

### 2.1 C2PA-as-a-Service Platform
Provider shall provide Customer with access to the C2PA-as-a-Service platform, including:

- **Manifest Generation**: Creation of C2PA-compliant manifests with cryptographic signatures
- **Remote Manifest Survival**: Guaranteed availability and verifiability of signed manifests
- **Content Verification**: Validation of C2PA manifests and content authenticity
- **Evidence Custody**: Immutable storage of compliance evidence with WORM retention
- **Compliance Reporting**: Automated generation of regional compliance reports

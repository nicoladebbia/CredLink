# Art. 28 Sub-processing Clauses

## Standard Sub-processor Clause

"Processor shall not appoint a Sub-processor without Controller's general written authorisation and advance notice; Processor remains liable; Sub-processors must be bound by terms no less protective than this DPA."

## Detailed Sub-processor Management

"Processor shall not engage any Sub-processor for processing Personal Data without Controller's prior general written authorization. Processor shall provide Controller with at least 30 days' advance notice of any intended changes concerning the addition or replacement of Sub-processors, thereby giving Controller the opportunity to object to such changes on reasonable data protection grounds. Processor shall remain fully liable to Controller for the performance of the Sub-processor's obligations. Processor shall ensure that Sub-processors are bound by written agreements imposing data protection obligations no less protective than those set out in this DPA."

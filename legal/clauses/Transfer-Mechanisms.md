# International Transfer Clauses

## Standard Transfer Clause

"For restricted transfers, Parties execute EU SCCs (2021/914) (Module as applicable) and, for UK transfers, the UK Addendum/IDTA."

## Detailed Transfer Mechanisms

"For transfers of Personal Data from the European Economic Area to third countries that do not ensure an adequate level of data protection as determined by the European Commission, the Parties shall execute the European Commission Standard Contractual Clauses as set out in Commission Implementing Decision (EU) 2021/914 of 4 June 2021, selecting the appropriate Module based on the roles of the Parties. For transfers of Personal Data from the United Kingdom to third countries, the Parties shall execute either the UK International Data Transfer Agreement (IDTA) or the UK Addendum to the EU Standard Contractual Clauses, as applicable and as required by UK data protection law."

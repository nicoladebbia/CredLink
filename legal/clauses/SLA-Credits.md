# SLA Credit Clauses

## Standard Service Credit Clause

"If Monthly Remote Survival < 99.9% or Verify API Uptime < 99.9%, Customer receives a service credit equal to X% of the monthly fee for the affected service. Credits are the sole and exclusive remedy for SLA breaches."

## Tiered Credit Structure

| Service Level | Credit Percentage |
|---------------|-------------------|
| 99.0% to <99.9% | 10% |
| 95.0% to <99.0% | 25% |
| 90.0% to <95.0% | 50% |
| <90.0% | 100% |

## Credit Application Process

"Service credits are automatically calculated and applied to Customer's next invoice within 5 business days of the end of the affected billing period."

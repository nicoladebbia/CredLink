## Overage Configuration

### Alert Thresholds
- **80% Alert**: Email notification + dashboard warning
- **95% Alert**: Critical email + webhook + in-app modal
- **100% Hard Cap**: Throttling (Starter) or overage billing (Growth/Scale)

### Proration Rules
- **Upgrades**: Immediate with proration
- **Downgrades**: Scheduled next billing cycle
- **Add-ons**: Immediate billing
- **Mid-term changes**: Daily proration calculation

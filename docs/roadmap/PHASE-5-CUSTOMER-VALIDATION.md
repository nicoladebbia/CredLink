## üî• FAILURE MODES & CONSEQUENCES

### Critical Failures (Phase 5 Fails)
1. **< 10 beta customers after 16 weeks**
   - Consequence: No market validation, unclear if problem exists
   - Recovery: Major pivot, rethink value proposition

2. **PMF score < 20% "very disappointed"**
   - Consequence: Built something nobody really needs
   - Recovery: Interviews to find actual pain point, rebuild

3. **Zero customers willing to pay**
   - Consequence: Free product, not a business
   - Recovery: Rethink pricing or find different customer segment

4. **Churn rate > 50% in first 90 days**
   - Consequence: Product doesn't deliver ongoing value
   - Recovery: Deep customer interviews, major product fixes

5. **No clear ideal customer profile**
   - Consequence: Can't scale acquisition, random customer base
   - Recovery: Customer segmentation analysis, pick niche

### Warning Signs (Fix Immediately)
- Beta customers not using product after first week
- No organic feature requests or feedback
- Customers describe product as "nice to have" not "must have"
- High support burden (>5 hours per customer per week)
- Customers asking for features that would completely change product
- Unable to articulate value proposition in one sentence
- No word-of-mouth or referrals happening

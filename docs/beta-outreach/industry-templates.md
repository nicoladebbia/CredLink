## Research Checklist

For each prospect:
- [ ] Company name and website
- [ ] Industry and sub-category
- [ ] Decision maker name
- [ ] Decision maker title
- [ ] Email (verified)
- [ ] LinkedIn profile URL
- [ ] Specific use case
- [ ] Priority level (High/Medium/Low)
- [ ] Company size/funding
- [ ] Recent news or context
- [ ] Source of information

## Time-Saving Tips

1. **Batch by industry** - Research all newsrooms at once
2. **Use LinkedIn Sales Navigator** - Advanced filters save hours
3. **Set up Google Alerts** - Get notified of relevant news
4. **Create email templates** - Reuse patterns you find
5. **Use Hunter.io bulk search** - Find multiple emails at once
6. **Save LinkedIn searches** - Rerun weekly for new prospects
7. **Use Clearbit Chrome extension** - Auto-enrichment
8. **Build a swipe file** - Save good examples
